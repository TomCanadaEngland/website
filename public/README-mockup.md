# Hero mockup image

Replace **`phone-mockup.png`** with your own app screenshot or mockup to show in the hero section (next to the headline and download buttons).

- **Path:** `public/phone-mockup.png`
- **Recommended:** A phone-sized screenshot of your app (e.g. 1170×2532 px or similar). It will be cropped to fit the phone frame with `object-cover`, so the top of the image is emphasized.
- **Format:** PNG or JPG (use `.png` or `.jpg` and update the `src` in `components/Hero.tsx` if you use a different filename).
